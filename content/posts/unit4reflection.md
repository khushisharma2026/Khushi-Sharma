### **Reflection**
In this unit, we worked on two projects, creating a chatbot and building a dashboard on metabase. Both the projects were fun since it was new for me to learn and we had to use our creativeness to design the projects.
I used resources from google and watched videos to learn about these tools. If i compare both the projects, building a chatbot was easier. It was just like how we play with building blocks and create something. But connecting the buttons to different sections was slightly confusing. Once I understood the concepts, it became easier to structure the chatbot. While designing a chatbot, I realized that it is not just about adding information, but organising it in a way such that it becomes easier for the user to navigate. This project was mostly individual based and group planning was not really required.
When we come to building dashboard, I felt it was a bit harder since it took me a lot of time to figure out what actually is a dashboard. The most challenging part for me was to think of a storyline. This is because the concept of making a story out of graphs was a completely new concept to me. To get ideas of storyline, I took example dashboards created by various people, online. I also got to learn about graphs from this particular project. Group planning was required for the project to avoid making similar storylines. Overall, both the projects were fun and I could learn a lot, but the main thing I learnt from these projects were how to create and design things in such a way that it will be easier for user to understand and grasp the information and navigate.
